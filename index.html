About Aquaculture

Today, 90% of large fish are already gone from the oceans. Twenty-five percent of wild stocks have collapsed. One projection asserts that – if fisheries management doesn’t change worldwide, then ocean fish stocks will be fully depleted by 2050. Fulfillment of this projection will have a catastrophic impact on human life and collapse multiple industries. Meanwhile, the human population as well as per capita seafood consumption continue to increase. Traditional aquaculture is growing rapidly, but has suffered from quality issues and limited opportunity for growth. The world needs better sources of protein that do not require fresh water or arable land.

About Us

Forever Oceans was launched as a spin-off of the Lockheed Martin Aquaculture Line of Business in 2014. Forever Oceans now combines leading technology with advanced biology, a comprehensive training program, and more, to offer investors the opportunity to license an end-to-end farm solution. We are proud of our heritage that includes a TIME Magazine Top 25 Inventions of the Year Award.

We aspire to grow our farm base in the warm water regions of the world. Implementing a sophisticated and environmentally responsible aquafarm requires substaintial investment, but it can be highly profitable. We are very selective about our licensing partners that operate Forever Oceans farms.

Our Approach

In the face of dwindling supply and rising global demand, Forever Oceans offers a reliable and sustainable aquaculture alternative. Forever Oceans provides complete aquaculture solutions that produce premium fish at globally competitive costs. Forever Ocean’s franchise-like offering includes robustly efficient systems, expert operational support and market channels that can deliver high yields of deliciously healthy fish to meet today’s demand and preserve the oceans for the next generation.

Forever Oceans has tackled the challenge of a more sophisticated technological and biological solution that allows fish to be grown where they would be naturally... the open ocean. In addition to protecting the environment, our automation and natural offshore conditions reduce operational risks and allow fish health to be maintained naturally. We are committed not only to preserving the environment, but also to leading the charge in voluntarily measuring ocean conditions to be able to show this quantitatively. We have shown that preserving nature can be good business. The rapid growth of our fish and low feed conversion rates are also very good for farm profitability.

We offer the most sophisticated approach to aquafarms in order to grow healthier, more sustainable fish in better conditions while keeping operations simple and reliable for our farm customers.
